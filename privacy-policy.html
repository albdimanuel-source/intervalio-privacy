02

Google AdMob advertising

We use Google AdMob to show ads in the free version of the app. AdMob may collect and use data to provide personalized or non-personalized ads, including:

📱Device identifiers (Advertising ID / IDFA)
🌐IP address and approximate location
⚙️Device information (model, OS version)
📊App usage data and interaction with ads

Learn more about how Google uses this data: policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized advertising in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking and disabling "Allow Apps to Request to Track".

You can also remove ads permanently with a one-time in-app purchase.

03

App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

In accordance with Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework, Intervalio will request your permission before accessing your device's Advertising Identifier (IDFA). A prompt will appear the first time you open the app.

You can change your decision at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → Intervalio.

If you deny tracking, AdMob will still show ads, but they will not be personalized based on your activity across other apps and websites.

05

Local data storage

All app data — your timer presets, custom intervals, preferences and settings — is stored locally on your device only using iOS UserDefaults and local storage.

This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party. Deleting the app will permanently remove all locally stored data.

06

Children's privacy

Intervalio is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you believe a child has provided personal information through our app, please contact us at intervalio.ios@gmail.com and we will promptly delete it.

07

GDPR — European users

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to access, correct, or delete your data.

Since Intervalio does not collect personal data directly, these rights primarily apply to data collected by Google AdMob. Google's data practices and your rights are described at policies.google.com/privacy.

For any GDPR-related requests, contact us at intervalio.ios@gmail.com.

08

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
