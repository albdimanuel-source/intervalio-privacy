FAQ

Frequently asked questions

QHow do I create a custom timer?
Tap "Create your own timer" at the bottom of the Timers list. Set your desired duration, name and icon, then save it. It will appear in your personal list ready to use.
QHow do I create a custom interval?
Go to the Intervals tab, choose any category (e.g. Tabata), then tap "Create custom interval". Set your work time, rest time and number of rounds.
QHow do I remove ads?
Tap the settings icon (⚙️) on the main screen and select "Remove Ads". It's a one-time purchase — no subscriptions, no recurring charges. Your family can share it too via Family Sharing.
QI purchased "Remove Ads" but ads are still showing. What do I do?
Tap "Restore Purchases" in the settings. If the issue persists, contact us at intervalio.ios@gmail.com and we'll sort it out immediately.
QDoes Intervalio work without internet?
Yes, completely. All timers and intervals work 100% offline. Internet is only used to load ads (if you haven't purchased the ad-free version).
QWill Intervalio run in the background?
Yes. Intervalio continues running when you switch to another app or lock your screen. You'll receive an alert when the timer finishes.
QHow do I delete a custom timer or interval?
Tap the Edit button in the top right corner of the list, then tap the red minus button next to the item you want to remove.
