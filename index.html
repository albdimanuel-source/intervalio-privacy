Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Intervalio is developed by Alberto Dimanuel. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

Intervalio is a timer app. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data directly. However, we use Google AdMob to display advertisements, which may collect certain data as described below.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to show ads in the app. AdMob may collect and use data to provide personalized or non-personalized ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID / IDFA)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • App usage data and interaction with ads

You can learn more about how Google uses data at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

To opt out of personalized advertising, you can adjust your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"

3. App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

In accordance with Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework, Intervalio will request your permission before accessing your device's Advertising Identifier (IDFA). You can change your decision at any time in your device Settings.

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

Intervalio does not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Health or fitness data
  • Payment information
  • Photos, camera, or microphone data

5. Data Storage

All app data (your timer presets, preferences, and settings) is stored locally on your device only using iOS UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

6. Children's Privacy

Intervalio is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

7. GDPR (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain rights regarding your personal data under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to access, correct, or delete your data. Since we do not collect personal data directly, these rights primarily apply to data collected by Google AdMob. Please refer to Google's privacy policy for more information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
